# PSE - 202602 - Group Assignment

## Project Title
MyCare (Appointment Booking System)

## Chosen Sustainable Development Goal
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being was chosen by our team to serve as the project’s foundation. This goal seeks to guarantee healthy lifestyles and enhances everyone’s wellbeing for all ages. Ineffective appointment scheduling remains to be a major obstacle to prompt medical access in healthcare systems around the world. Legacy appointment scheduling system frequently have:
* Limited accessibility for patients
*	Inadequate communication between medical professionals and patients
*	Inefficient use of resources
*	Inability to integrate with medical records
*	Minimal transparency in appointment availability

In order to overcome these obstacles, MyCare provides a digital platform that simplifies every step of the appointment process, from scheduling and confirmation to documentation and follow-up.

## Project Description
MyCare is a digital healthcare management system designed to help patients and healthcare professionals manage medical appointments more efficiently. Through the system, patients can easily book appointments with doctors using an online platform without needing to go to the clinic just to make an appointment. At the same time, it saves time and makes the process more convenient for patients. The system will provide reminder notifications to patients before their scheduled appointments so that they do not forget about their consultations. Doctors can use the system to view appointments and manage patient visits in a more organized way, while administrators can manage user accounts and monitor system activities.

## Expected Outcomes
The performance of the MyCare system is expected to improve the efficiency of healthcare appointment management. Patients will be able to schedule medical consultations more easily and receive reminders about their appointments. Doctors will have better control over their schedules, which can help them manage patient consultations more effectively. Additionally, the system can help reduce missed appointments and improve the overall healthcare service experience for both patients and healthcare professionals.

## Alignment with Chosen SDG
MyCare supports Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good health and Well-Being by helping improve access to healthcare services through digital technology. By making it easier for patients to schedule and attend medical appointments, the system encourages individuals to seek medical care when needed and maintain better health practices. This contributes to promoting healthier lives and improving the overall wellbeing of communities.
