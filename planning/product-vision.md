## 1. Vision Statement

For patients seeking medical care at a hospital who deals with lenghthy wait times and limited access to an appointment booking services. The MyCare web-based platform is an online appointment booking system that allows round-the-clock booking, reduces queue times and provide transparent scheduling. Unlike current walk-in-only system or calling to schedule an appointment, our product offers instantaneous booking confirmation, real-time queue visibility, flexible appointment scheduling and automated reminders, making healthcare access predictable and convenient.
