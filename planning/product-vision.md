## 1. Vision Statement

For patients, doctors and hospital administrators
Who have trouble using outdated, ineffective appointment scheduling systems
The MyCare software solution
Is a complete platform for scheduling and managing appointments
That makes it possible to plan appointments easily, check schedules in real-time, document medical record electronically and automated notifications
Unlike conventional legacy systems with manual procedures and limited accessibility
Our product increases healthcare efficiency, decreases waiting times and enhances patient experience through modern, user-centred design.

---
## 2. Problem Statement

In many healthcare facilities, patients often face difficulties when trying to schedule medical appointments. Normal appointment systems are usually handled manually, which can lead to long waiting period and confusion in scheduling. Patients may also forget their appointment dates, which can lead to delay their treatment and affect their health. Meanwhile, doctors may find it challenging to manage multiple appointments and patient records easily. Because of these problems, there is a need for a simple digital system that can help improve appointment management and make healthcare services more reachable.

## 4. Product Overview

MyCare is a digital healthcare management system designed to help patients and healthcare professionals manage medical appointments more efficiently. Through the system, patients can easily book appointments with doctors using an online platform without needing to go to the clinic just to make an appointment. At the same time, it saves time and makes the process more convenient for patients. The system will provide reminder notifications to patients before their scheduled appointments so that they do not forget about their consultations. Doctors can use the system to view appointments and manage patient visits in a more organized way, while administrators can manage user accounts and monitor system activities.

---
## 5. Value Proposition

The MyCare system provides a convenient and efficient solution for managing healthcare appointments. By allowing patients to book appointments online, the system helps reduce wasting time and makes healthcare more available. Reminder notifications also help patients remember their consultations, which can reduce missed appointments. For healthcare providers, the system helps improve schedule management and makes it easier to coordinate patient care. Overall, MyCare aims to create a more organized and reliable healthcare service experience.

## 7. Expected Outcomes

The performance of the MyCare system is expected to improve the efficiency of healthcare appointment management. Patients will be able to schedule medical consultations more easily and receive reminders about their appointments. Doctors will have better control over their schedules, which can help them manage patient consultations more effectively. Additionally, the system can help reduce missed appointments and improve the overall healthcare service experience for both patients and healthcare professionals.
